How To Play

Easy Game Rules

1. Start the game

Choose local play, computer mode, or online room. Then shuffle and deal the cards.

2. Cards are shared

One starting player gets 5 cards. Every other player gets 4 cards.

3. Pass one card

On your turn, choose one card from your hand and pass it to the next player.

4. Make four same cards

Your goal is to collect 4 cards of the same name, like 4 Ram cards or 4 Sita cards.

5. First player wins

As soon as one player gets 4 matching cards, that round ends and the winner is shown.

6. Play again

Use the Play Again button to start the next round. The session score keeps track of wins.

About The Game

What Is 16 Parchi Dhup?

16 Parchi Dhup is a simple passing card game for friends and family. Some players also search for it as 16 Parchi Dhap or 16 Parchi card game. In every round, players pass one card at a time and try to collect four cards of the same type before anyone else.

Is 16 Parchi Dhup and 16 Parchi Dhap the same game?

Yes, many people use both spellings when searching for this card game online.

Can I play 16 Parchi online with friends?

Yes. You can create an online room, share the invite link, join from different devices, chat, and play together.

Can I play alone?

Yes. Use the computer mode to play as one human player against three computer players.
